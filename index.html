About Us

The Black Maternal Health Scholarship was created to raise awareness and support solutions for Black Maternal Health. We parnter with community organizations to empower studnets entering healthcare to close gaps in access, quality, and outcomes.

About the Scholarship

Black women are three times more likely to die during or after childbirth in comparison to their white counterparts. Underrepresented communities are constantly put at a disadvantage due to the lack of diversity in our nation’s healthcare field. This scholarship fund hopes to alleviate the financial burden affecting this unfortunate reality with the award of $500 to five graduating seniors intending to pursue a post-secondary education in medicine or the health sciences. Additionally, all scholarship recipients will be awarded with a free computer for their college journey.

Application Timeline

  1. Application Opens: May 1, 2026
  2. Application Deadline: July 1, 2026
  3. Notifications: Applicants will be contacted within the second week of August regardless of their decision.

Who Should Apply

  • Must be a student of color.
  • Must be graduating from high school in the spring of 2025.
  • Must be enrolled to attend a 4-year university in the fall of 2025.
  • Must intend to pursue a career in the healthcare field.
  • Must meet one of the requirements from the financial checklist.
  • Must be Pell-eligible.
